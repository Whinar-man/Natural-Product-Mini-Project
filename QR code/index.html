Abstract

This project evaluates a herbal aphrodisiac decoction prepared from Sphenocentrum jollyanum, Paullinia pinnata, and Cinnamomum zeylanicum. Drawing on traditional knowledge and recent scientific findings, the study aims to evaluate the biochemical composition, pharmacological effects, and safety profiles of the above herbal plants known for their efficacy in improving libido, sexual performance, and reproductive health. Through a multidisciplinary approach combining phytochemical analysis, and in vivo studies, the research seeks to validate the therapeutic claims of these plants.

Constituents

StrongMan mixture is a decoction preparation of, Roots of Sphenocentrum jollyanum Roots of Paullinia pinnata and Roots of Cinnamomum zeylanicum. These plants are known to have some constituents that aid in improving libido and sexual performance.

Therapeutic Use

StrongMan Mixture is for ORAL USE ONLY. The decoction mixture was formulated and tested on lab rats following standard research protocols and is confirmed for sexual performance and libido improvement in both men and women. The reommended dose is 30ml when required.

Caution

  • Not recommended for Preganant and Lactating Mothers.
  • Not recommended for Children below age 18.
  • Store In a Cool dry place away from Children.

Product Handling & Safety

Stable under refrigeration and keep away from Light. Sodium benzoate ensured preservation with No toxic compounds detected.
