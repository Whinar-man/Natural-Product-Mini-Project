Abstract

This study evaluates a herbal aphrodisiac decoction prepared from Sphenocentrum jollyanum, Paullinia pinnata, and Cinnamomum zeylanicum. Tests confirmed quality, safety, and effectiveness, with observed increases in testosterone and sexual behavior in male rats. Shelf-life estimated at 4–6 weeks under refrigeration.

Introduction

The herbal decoction was designed to improve libido and sexual performance using plants with traditional and scientific backing. The combination was selected for their individual aphrodisiac and physiological benefits.

Literature Review

  • Sphenocentrum jollyanum: CNS stimulation, testosterone increase
  • Paullinia pinnata: Vascular relaxation, saponins for testosterone
  • Cinnamomum zeylanicum: Blood flow enhancement and antioxidants

Materials and Methodology

Fresh plant roots were dried, powdered, and mixed in 3:1:1 ratio. Decoction was prepared in water and preserved with alcohol or sodium benzoate. Organoleptic and phytochemical tests confirmed identity and bioactivity.

Results

  • pH: 5.4
  • Color: Straw
  • Odor: Cinnamon-like
  • Bioactives retained: saponins, alkaloids, flavonoids, tannins
  • In vivo: Higher testosterone, increased sexual behavior

Discussion

The formulation retained its key actives, and in vivo tests validated its efficacy. The combination proved to enhance male sexual behavior through hormonal and vascular pathways.

Product Handling & Safety

Stable under refrigeration. Sodium benzoate and alcohol ensured preservation. No toxic compounds detected.

Conclusion

This herbal decoction is a promising aphrodisiac remedy. Its preparation method ensures both efficacy and safety, suitable for further study and potential commercialization.
